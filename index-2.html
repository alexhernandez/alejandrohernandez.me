Challenge

CSUN has a suite of products all designed and developed by different organizations across the university. A major problem across all groups was the lack of branding consistency, continued re-development of interface elements and the absence of a proper mobile experience for every new product. Our vision was to create a design language that could streamline the experience for users and unify product teams across multiple product lines.
